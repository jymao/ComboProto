Rough prototype of a 2D brawler with combo attacks.

The player has three attacks (up, side, down) that each have a stronger, slower variant. The attacks are executed by holding the direction and pressing the attack key.

The player has a double jump and can attack in mid-air. Hold the jump button to increase air time and press the down arrow to fall faster.


Controls

Movement: Left and right arrow keys

Jump: Spacebar

Weak attack (+ Arrow): Z

Strong attack (+ Arrow): X
